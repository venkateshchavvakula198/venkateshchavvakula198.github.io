WELCOME TO OUR CHARITY

SELF SERVICE FOUNDATION IS ONE OF INDIA’S LARGEST INDEPENDENT AND YOUTH VOLUNTEER NON-PROFIT ORGANISATIONS.

OUR VOLUNTEERS EDUCATE AND MENTOR CHILDREN FROM ORPHANAGES, SLUM AND VILLAGE COMMUNITY CENTERS ACROSS THE COUNTRY TO GIVE THEM A BETTER FUTURE, WHICH WILL BENEFIT THEM AS WELL AS OUR COUNTRY. THE LEARNING EXPERIENCE IS MUTUAL – OUR VOLUNTEERS GAIN PERSPECTIVE AND THE EXPERIENCE TO MOULD THEMSELVES INTO TOMORROW’S LEADERS.
